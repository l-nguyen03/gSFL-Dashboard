Welcome to the Demonstrator

This is the Demonstrator of gSFL. The Process of trading with Goods or Data can be simulated on the following pages. This Process works like this:

  • DT-management: Check the latest DT data
  • Assets: Upload your Asset
  • Policy: Define the trade-conditions
  • Contract Definition: Create a new Contract Definition for one or more Assets and set trade-conditions through the Policy
  • Catalog: Contract Definitions of all Participants can be negotiated
  • Closed Contracts: See all accepted Contracts and optionally transfer them to your Database
  • Received Data: View all data received from other participants
